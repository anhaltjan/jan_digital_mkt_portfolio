Strategical Viewpoint

Jan coined a personal term for strategy called "repeatable micro-dynamism" which means working hard on small repeatable tasks that add up to huge wins long-term. For example, if the goal is to improve followers/subscribers; have a daily or biweekly goal of 1%. If done correct, the end result will be massive and repeatable the next year. This allows you to outcompete competition, boost moral, moreover simplify tasks for all team members.

Social Media Content Strategy (simplified)

  • Define your long & short term goals
  • Let's define all your target markets, inside define the buyer personas with as much detail as possible.
  • Based ony goals & defined personas create a strategy to target your leads.
  • Using previous & future data to find what content hits hard & pump it up to increase leads & engagements.
    1. quantity > quality content (post a lot)
    2. data analysis (find what content works)
    3. create quality posts (pump up the good content)
    4. Post quality everyday
  • Turn content that improved organic growth & turn it into paid advertisment
  • Collect data, report results, improve the process & repeat.

Background

From 2012-2019 Jan Anhalt opened "JYAN Denim" which specialized in designing, tailoring Japanese denim jeans. Jan gained notariety for extremely high quality and extremely detailed products. Because of loss of a loved one, Jan decided to close his business and pursue his dreams living in Japan and becoming fluent in Japanese. Since 2020 Jan has been living in Japan as an ESL instructor, in his off-time Jan learned front-end development and UI/UX design. Now he resides in Tokyo and enjoys weight-lifting, bouldering and enjoying cigars with coffee or drinks with friends.

In the future Jan has a goal to create runway fashion collections using music that has inspired him through out the years.
