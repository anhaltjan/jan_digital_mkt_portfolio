Jan Anhalt is a Canadian designer and fashion consultant who honed his skills in designing, tailoring, sewing menswear, and consulting in formal wear. Despite possessing these skills, there was one significant skill he lacked - marketing.

When he first founded his company, he had very good word of mouth and a reputation for creating quality pieces for his clients. However, his struggle with perfectionism and low self-confidence to market himself prevented his company from scaling, which ultimately led to its failure.

Due to always being fearful of putting himself out there, which held him back in his twenties from seizing many personal and financial opportunities for growth. His life lesson taught him he needed to change, he decided to turn his weakness into strength. He made a conscious effort to have thicker skin and grow his confidence, furthering his skills.

Now committed to becoming the best in the industry, Jan Anhalt is leveraging his skills in menswear and newfound expertise in digital marketing to help companies reach male audiences and improve their organic growth on social media.
