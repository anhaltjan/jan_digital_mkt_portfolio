As an entrepreneur and salesman, I honed my skills in designing, tailoring, sewing, and selling menswear. Despite my proficiency in these areas, there was one significant thing that I failed to implement - marketing. When I first founded my company, I was introverted and struggled with low self-confidence which lead my company to failure.

As a perfectionist, I was always fearful of putting myself out there, which held me back from seizing many personal and financial growth opportunities. Realizing that I needed to turn this weakness into a strength, I made a conscious decision to pursue digital marketing.

I am now committed to becoming the best in the industry, leveraging my skills in design, tailoring, sales and my newfound expertise in digital marketing to reach a wider audience and propel myself forward. I am confident that by focusing on this area, I can overcome my past challenges and create a more successful and sustainable future for my own and others' businesses and/or endeavours.

I am determined to make a positive impact on companies and industries that serve male customers through digital marketing. With a deep understanding of the power of digital marketing, I am passionate about utilizing my skills and expertise to help these organizations thrive in the digital world. My focus on serving male customers allows me to tailor digital marketing strategies that resonate with their unique needs and challenges.

I am committed to developing innovative solutions that help these companies connect with their target audience in meaningful ways, ultimately driving business growth and success. My dedication, creativity, and passion for digital marketing make me an invaluable asset to any organization that seeks to make a positive impact on male customers. I am excited about the opportunity to contribute to positive change in these industries through digital marketing and to help them achieve their goals in the digital landscape.
